About Barbara

I am a former Assistant District Attorney with extensive trial and appellate experience. I was president of the Orange County Bar Association in 2010-2011 and I am a former president of the Women's Bar Association of Orange and Sullivan Counties. I am an active member of the Orange County Collaborative Divorce practice group and I am a court appointed attorney for children. I lecture for the Parents Apart program, an educational program for parents going through divorce or separation.

I have been attorney advisor for the Cardozo Law School trial advocacy program and I have taught continuing legal education courses in area of business valuation, pension distribution, new issues in Family Law and basic matrimonial practice. I have been formally trained in both mediation and collaborative divorce.

I am knowledgable about the law, hardworking and I genuinely care about each of my clients.

In my matrimonial/family law practice I handle matters in Rockland, Westchester, Orange, Sullivan, Dutchess and Ulster Counties.

I handle appellate matters throughout New York. I have perfected over 200 appeals in the Appellate Division and I have briefed and argued several matters in the Court of Appeals. I have been influential in crystalizing the appellate law on issues ranging from distribution of FDNY and NYPD pensions, survivor pension benefits for non-titled spouses, distribution of 9/11 compensation funds, college contributions after divorce, distribution of marital debt, joint custody, and grandparent rights. I have also been sucessful in arguing civil appeals on issues ranging from oil spills to water use easements. I have argued a full range of criminal issues from prosecutorial misconduct, to sex abuse corroboration, to DWI blood test orders and admissability of statements and evidence.

Professional, effective, compassionate, but firm

5.0 stars

Posted by Liza
February 16, 2017

When my attempt at a collaborative divorce failed, Barbara Strauss was recommended to me as an alternative for a court-savvy yet not overly savage attorney. I wanted a firm hand, but more of a consultative situation. I wanted to maintain as much dignity and humanity in the trying proceedings as I possibly could. I found working with Ms. Strauss and her staff, especially her Paralegal Ms. Jennifer Lopez, both productive and pleasant. The team was personable and responsive and offered creative solutions in my complex situation. Ms. Strauss was interested in my circumstances not only professionally, but as a fellow human being. I found compassion and understanding, yet a firm opinion and solid professional advice. I am using the firm now, will use them again, and am happy to recommend their services to women and men in need. While Barbara Strauss's services did not come cheap, the firm did not charge me excessively and tried to save me money everywhere they could. Overall, I feel that my money was well invested as I was paying for future peace and comfort.
